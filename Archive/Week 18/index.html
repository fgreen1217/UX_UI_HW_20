Hello

My name is Finn Green, an investment advisor turned UX/UI designer.

From running a photography business to analyzing data for stocks, UX/UI has offered the perfect blend of my creativity and research developing skills.

In my short amount of time as a designer I have developed strong skills throughout the design life cycle, with a main focus and strengths in workflow management, research, branding, and prototyping. Coupled along with my highly organized, diligent, and problem solving nature, I am confident I can take on any design challenge, big or small.

I'm passionate about creating digital experiences that are not only visually stunning but also intuitive and easy to use.

MY UX/UI Skills

Research

Looking at the industry as a whole first and the market share leaders. Learning the strengths and weaknesses of all so I can begin to develop the “problem”. Then I dive into the user statistics, studying trends to get initial reading of potential hangups and success points to begin shaping a solution.

Branding

Ideating themes and styles through mood boards, heuristic evaluations, and feature prioritizations on Miro and Figjam. I always enjoy brainstorming with a team or solo, putting together the basis for what could be. From inspiration to implementation, the app or site’s brand (or rebrand) can come to life in the style guides.

Prototyping

From sketches to hi-fi prototyping and the iterations and testings that go on in between, I have developed strong skills in Figma to achieve the visions I set out in the projects at hand.
